The mission of Obong University is to provide an environment suitable for academic and moral development that will guarantee the production of globally competitive graduates through cutting edge technology for societal benefit and self reliance.

On the other hand, the vision of Obong University is to be an academic centre of excellence that will provide an enabling environment for nurturing students who will excel in their areas of dicipline thereby attaining maximum personal and intellectual development for the benefit of society.
