Undergraduate Programme

Obong University provides quality undergraduate Programmes in Natural & Applied Sciences, Management Sciences, Social Sciences and Arts & Humanities in an environment of academic excellence. The University also offers opportunities for spiritual growth and encouragement for entrepreneurial skills development.



General Admission Requirements

Admission into Obong University is through the Joint Admission and Matriculation Board (JAMB) and Joint Universities Preliminary Examination Board (JUPEB).

For Direct Entry (DE) admission, candidates should (in addition to SSCE credit passes) obtain a minimum of two (2) A�Level (or its equivalence) passes in relevant subjects, or possess NCE, A/Level, OND/NABTEB or HND with credit/merit passes or possess a good First Degree in relevant field as the case may be.
